Assessment Model

How you are graded

Your final grade is made up of three components. Consistent effort through the term is rewarded.

40%
Weekly Continuous Assessment
Short weekly tests on each unit. Covers vocabulary, grammar, and comprehension. Taken every Friday in the student portal.
50%
End of Level Final Exam
Comprehensive exam at the end of each level. 50 questions across all units. Must score 70%+ to progress and receive certification.
10%
Class Contribution
Awarded for daily engagement — logging in, completing lessons, participating in live classes, and using the AI coach.
Minimum pass mark: 70% across all components. Students who score below 70% may retake the final exam once before repeating the level. The AI English Coach session contributes 20% of the final exam component for language proficiency.
