Assessment Model

How you are graded

Consistent effort through the term is always rewarded. Three components build your final grade.

40%
Weekly Continuous Assessment
Short weekly CAT tests on each unit. Covers vocabulary, grammar and comprehension. Taken every week in your student portal.
50%
End of Level Final Exam
Comprehensive exam at the end of each level. Must score 70%+ to progress and receive your CEFR-aligned certificate.
10%
Class Contribution
Awarded for daily engagement — logging in, completing lessons, using the AI coach and participating in live classes.
Minimum pass mark: 70% across all components. Students who score below 70% may retake the final exam once before repeating the level.
